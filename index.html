Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Eurokids Gatthaghar, Discovery Montessori, Lincoln Int'l School ("we", "our", or "us") app developed by Techyatra Labs. Designed for parents and teachers in Kathmandu, Nepal, to access attendance tracking, billing, homework management, and study materials with ease, our app respects the privacy and security of all users—parents, teachers, and children. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and your choices regarding your data when you use the Euro Kids Gatthaghar mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Your name, email, phone number, and role (parent or teacher).
  • Child Information: Child’s name, date of birth, grade, and attendance records.
  • Billing Details: Payment method, billing address, transaction history (processed securely via third-party).
  • Homework & Study Materials: Homework submissions, grades, teacher comments.
  • Device & Usage Data: Device type, OS version, IP address, usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery & Improvement: To provide and optimize features.
  • Notifications & Communication: Attendance alerts, billing reminders, support.
  • Payment Facilitation: Process subscriptions and refunds.
  • Analytics & Insights: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: Explicit consent when registering or using features.
  • Contractual Necessity: Fulfill service agreement for paid features.
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with laws (e.g., financial records).

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Third-Party Providers: Payment, hosting, analytics under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: Comply with laws, court orders, governmental requests.
  • No Sale of Personal Data: We never sell or rent personal information.

5. Children’s Privacy & COPPA/GDPR Compliance

We collect children’s data only with parental or teacher direction and verifiable consent, in line with COPPA and GDPR guidelines. For data subjects under 13, we verify parental authorization.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our App may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, improve performance, and analyze traffic. You may disable non-essential cookies via device settings.

7. Data Security and Retention

We implement encryption, secure authentication, and regular audits to protect your data. We retain data as long as necessary; deletion requests are processed within 30 days, except for legally required records.

8. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and authorities within 72 hours of discovery.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Correction: View/update your data via the App.
  • Data Portability: Request export of your data.
  • Deletion & Restriction: Request deletion or restriction of processing.
  • Marketing Opt‑Out: Email support@techyatralabs.com.

10. External Links and Third‑Party Sites

Our App may link to third‑party sites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Please review their policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy for practice, feature, or legal changes. We will notify you at least 15 days before implementation.
