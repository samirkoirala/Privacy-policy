Introduction

Welcome to Techyatralabs ("we", "our", or "us"). 5. Children’s Privacy & COPPA/GDPR Compliance

Some of our apps may involve the collection of data from children under 13. We ensure compliance with COPPA and GDPR by requiring verifiable parental or guardian consent before collecting such data.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our apps may use cookies or similar technologies for preferences, performance improvements, and analytics. Users may manage preferences via device settings.

We implement encryption, authentication, and access controls to protect your data. Data is retained as long as necessary for its intended purpose unless otherwise requested or legally required.

If a data breach occurs, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours, in compliance with applicable laws.
